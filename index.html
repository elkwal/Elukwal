Types of triangles

  1. Equilateral triangle
  2. Isosceles triangle
  3. Scalene triangle
  4. Obtuse triangle
  5. Acute triangle
  6. Right angled triangle

Properties of equilateral triangle

  • All sides are equal
  • All angles are equal

Properties of isosceles triangle

  • Two angles are equal
  • Two sides are equal

Properties of acute angles

  • All angles are less than 90'

Properties of an obtuse angle

  • One angle greater than 90'

Properties of a right angled triangle

  • One angle equal to 90'
